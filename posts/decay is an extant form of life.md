Tumblr poetry goes so hard sometimes. Back in 2018 @personsonable wrote *you cannot kill me in a way that matters* and *decay exists as an extant form of life*. 

It was a shitpost about mushrooms, but *damn.* 

We live by sucking life from other things, from our earliest formless weeks in utero, leeching calcium from our mothers' teeth to build our bones. We die, and the cells we built are devoured by other beings, our borrowed atoms returned to the collective store and used again by a thousand thousand new lives. 

It's unsettling. 

It's beautiful. 

I hope I become a [[stump|tree]].
